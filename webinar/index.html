The Guru Bullshit Detector

🚩
Income Screenshots Without Context
"$47,000 last month!" but they don't show expenses, refunds, or whether it was a one-time spike. Real entrepreneurs share profit margins, not just revenue porn.
🎪
Fake Urgency Everywhere
"Only 47 spots left!" (same number for 3 weeks). "Price goes up at midnight!" (has been going up for 6 months). Real scarcity doesn't need countdown timers.
🤡
Teaching What They Don't Practice
They teach affiliate marketing but their only business is selling affiliate marketing courses. It's courses all the way down. Find someone with a real business.

Empire Building Strategies That Actually Work

No bullshit. No fake promises. Just proven methods that build real wealth.

01
The Authority Content Framework
Create content that solves real problems. 80% value, 20% promotion. Build trust first, monetize second. When people think of your niche, they should think of you first.
02
Email List Empire Building
Email converts 3-5x better than social media. Build your list from day one. Create lead magnets that provide immediate value and establish your expertise.

The Honest Review System
Actually buy and test products for 30+ days. Share real results, including what sucks. Build bonuses that make buying through your link a no-brainer.

Pick 2-3 platforms maximum. Master them completely before expanding. Own your primary platform (blog/email), rent space on others (social media).

Build systems that work without you. Document processes, create templates, and eventually hire team members to handle execution while you focus on strategy.
